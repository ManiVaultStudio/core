Cytosplore Viewer
Version: 5.0
Interactive visual analytics for the exploration of single cell and spatial transcriptomics data

Cytosplore Viewer is an interactive visual analytics system for the exploration of single cell and spatial transcriptomics data published in the Allen Cell Types Database and for a number of the curated, authorative data resources of the Brain Initiative Cell Atlas Network (BICAN), in particular the Human and Mammalian Brain Atlas. Cytosplore Viewer is designed to leverage fast local compute and compressed data access for the linked exploration of multi-modal spatial and single cell datasets.
